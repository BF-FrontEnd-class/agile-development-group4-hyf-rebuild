Your new career starts here

HackYourFuture is afree 9-month front end web development programfor refugees, newcomers and 'anderstalige' jobseekers passionate about code and web development. You don't need any technical background to start, on'y loads of motivation.

If you want to kickstart your career in the IT sector, take your chance! Women are strongly encouraged to apply!

Who can apply?

We teach motivated refugees and newcomers with limited access to education or the labor market who want to start their career in web development. The course is open to anyone staying in Belgium. Women are strongly encouraged to apply!

  • ✅ You're+18 years

  • ✅ You'reextremely motivated

  • ✅ You speak at an intermediate level.

  • ✅ You can study a minimum of 25 hours per week

  • ✅ You are recognized as a refugee

  • ✅ You are a migrant with limited access to education or the labor market
